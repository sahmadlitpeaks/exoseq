About ExoSeq®

Our mission is to give every patient a clearer answer

We exist to help clinicians resolve diagnostic odysseys faster — combining clinical-grade exome sequencing with AI-assisted phenotyping and the judgment of board-certified geneticists.

Our approach

Clinical rigor meets continuous learning

ExoSeq® reports are produced by a combined human + AI workflow that's purpose-built for clinical use. Every sample passes documented QC; every variant is reviewed against the latest evidence; every report is signed out by clinicians.

  • CAP-accredited laboratory partners
  • Proprietary bioinformatics with HPO-aware ranking
  • Independent dual-review of every reported variant
  • Lifetime reanalysis as evidence evolves

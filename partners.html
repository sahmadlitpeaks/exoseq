Partners

A network built on clinical trust

ExoSeq® collaborates with accredited laboratories, hospitals, research institutions and prescribing clinicians across the globe to deliver consistent, high-quality genetic answers.

Accredited laboratories

CAP- and ISO 15189-accredited partner laboratories handle DNA extraction, library preparation and sequencing under strict QC protocols.

Hospitals & clinics

Direct integrations with hospital genetics departments, oncology centers and pediatric units around the world.

Research institutions

Joint translational programs in rare disease, hereditary cancer and pharmacogenomics with leading universities.

Prescribing clinicians

Specialty physicians who collaborate directly with our genetic team on case interpretation and follow-up.

Distribution partners

Regional partners enable localized billing, sample logistics and language-specific patient support.

Industry collaborations

Strategic partnerships with biotech, pharma and AI companies to keep our pipeline at the leading edge.

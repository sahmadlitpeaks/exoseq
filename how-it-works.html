The ExoSeq® Process

From request to clinical answer

A transparent five-step workflow built around the realities of clinical practice — including the people, science and quality systems behind every report.

  1. 1

    Search

    Identify the right report via ExoSearch using gene, condition or specialty.

  2. 2

    Request

    Submit the request — directly or through your prescribing physician.

  3. 3

    Sample

    Customer Care guides DNA collection through an accredited partner lab.

  4. 4

    Report

    Geneticists & clinicians review every variant before sign-out.

  5. 5

    Support

    Optional counseling, reanalysis and ongoing case management.

Step 2 & 3 · Request and Sample

A clinician-friendly intake

The request form captures only what's needed — phenotype, family history, prior testing — and our Customer Care team coordinates collection logistics with the partner laboratory of your choice.

  • Whole blood (EDTA), buccal, saliva or extracted DNA
  • Pediatric and trio testing fully supported
  • Documented chain-of-custody at every step
  • Multi-language consent forms available

Quality at every step

Each sample passes ≥ 5 QC checkpoints before sequencing.

Step 4 · Analysis & report

AI assistance, human accountability

Variants are filtered, prioritized and matched to the patient's phenotype using our proprietary pipelines and HPO ontologies — but every report is signed out by a board-certified team of geneticists and clinicians.

  • ACMG/AMP 2015 classification with specialty refinements
  • Phenotype-driven prioritization (HPO + clinical notes)
  • Independent dual-review of every reported variant
  • Clear separation of primary, secondary and uncertain findings
